Money Hub is a simple, free, and personal budgeting tracker designed to help track income, expenses, and bills without complicated features.

## Features
- Add income or expense entries  
- Choose daily or weekly entry mode  
- Basic spending categories  
- Custom monthly bills input  
- Automatic calculation of money left  
- History log of all entries  
- Works on phone and computer  
- 100% free and offline (saved in your browser)

## How to Use
1. Open the website  
2. Go to **Settings** and enter your total monthly bills  
3. Use **Add Entry** to log income or spending  
4. View everything from the **Dashboard**

## Privacy
All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage.  
Nothing is sent to any server.

## Purpose
Built to help stop living paycheck to paycheck with a simple system instead of overwhelming finance apps.
